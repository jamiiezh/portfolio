Project Overview

The objective of this project is to develop a better understanding of game design and realize the importance of certain level of player interactions as well as challenge level in order to maintain a balance game. In addition, the skills of conducting playtesting sessions including player surveys and player observations are learned to further improve the game.

Project Evaluation

From this project, I realized the importance of playtesting. Game designer could not only observe how people learn to play their game in a way to reflect themselves, but also hear how other people think about the game. By conducting playtesting session, I learned that making a good rule sheet is critical as it is the first step that people learn about the game, a short and clear rule sheet could boost the interest of people. In addition, a good game does not come from only one iteration, several iterations are needed to get feedback and refine the game.
